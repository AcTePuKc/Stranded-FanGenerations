Filter Name: Survivor Cinema
Theme: Cinematic post-collapse realism
Color: Desaturated natural tones with golden hour highlights and muted shadows
Camera: Slow handheld shots with soft depth of field, occasional dolly-ins
Film Stock: Digital film with subtle grain and organic lens flares
Lighting: Soft, volumetric, with atmospheric haze and sun peeking through ruins or clouds
Vibe: Solemn, reflective, emotionally grounded
Motion: Wind movement, dust particles, flickering lights, environmental shifts (leaves, smoke, clothing)
